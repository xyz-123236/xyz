正则表达式:
判断某值是否满足正则表达式：
	Pattern.matches(regex, value): Pattern.matches("^[1-9]\\d*$", "a270009998")
	string.matches(regex): "a270009998".matches("^[1-9]\\d*$")
匹配正则表达式的内容：
	Pattern p = Pattern.compile("\\(.*?\\)");
	Matcher m = p.matcher(value);
	while(m.find()){
		System.out.println(m.group().substring(1, m.group().length()-1));
	}
